Server Rules

1️⃣ Respect Everyone

  • ✅ No hate speech, racism, sexism, or personal attacks.
  • ✅ Be kind and respectful at all times.

2️⃣ No Toxicity

  • ✅ Keep trash talk friendly.
  • ✅ No spamming, trolling, or drama.

3️⃣ Voice Chat Rules 🎤

  • ✅ No loud mic spamming / ear-rape.
  • ✅ Respect others in VC.

4️⃣ No Cheats or Hacks 🚫

  • ✅ Strictly no cheating or exploiting.
  • ✅ Sharing cheats = instant ban.

5️⃣ Keep Things Clean 🧹

  • ✅ Use the right channels.
  • ✅ No NSFW, illegal, or harmful content.

6️⃣ Respect Staff 👑

  • ✅ Mods/Admins have the final say.
  • ✅ Don’t argue with staff in public chat.

7️⃣ No Random Ads 📢

  • ✅ Self-promotion only in the right channel (if allowed).
  • ✅ No DM advertising.

8️⃣ Gaming Spirit 🎮

  • ✅ Help new players.
  • ✅ Keep the server fun for everyone!

9️⃣ Follow Discord ToS 📖

  • ✅ You must follow Discord’s Terms of Service.
  • ⚠️ Breaking rules = warning, mute, or ban.

🔥 Final Note

Have fun, play fair, and enjoy gaming together!
